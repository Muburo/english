## 2. 今回のフォーカス弱点：文の途中で構文が崩壊する

今回最も顕著だったのは、**文を始めたものの途中で文法構造が保てなくなり、断片の羅列になるパターン**。特に理由の説明や結論で顕著。

| 実際の発話 | → | 完成形 |
|-----------|---|--------|
| "Building a house costs you local things like maintenance, and mental." | → | **"Owning a house costs you a lot in maintenance and takes a mental toll."** / **"Having a house means dealing with expensive upkeep and constant stress."** |
| "I definitely recommend a living up is a living apartment complex." | → | **"I definitely recommend living in an apartment complex."** / **"I'd strongly suggest choosing an apartment over a house."** |
| "Moving to another place gives me a sense of notice and I have another habit. Management awareness." | → | **"Moving gives me a sense of novelty and a chance to build new habits."** / **"Relocating helps me reset my lifestyle — new routines, new awareness."** |

**覚えるルール**: 文を始める前に **「主語 + 動詞 + 何を」** の骨格を3語で頭に決めてから話す。特に recommend / suggest の後は動名詞（~ing）で続ける。骨格さえ決まれば、途中で崩壊しにくい。
