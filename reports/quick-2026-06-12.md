## 1. 今回のトップ3（言えなかった表現）

### ① 「朝食は食べず、プロテインシェイクだけ飲む」
- **言いたかったこと**: 基本的に朝食は食べない。ドリンクタイプのプロテインだけ飲む。
- **実際の発話**: "I drink protein, a cup of protein every day. But I don't have anything for that."
- **完成形の例文2つ**:
  - "I usually don't eat breakfast; I just have a protein shake in the morning."
  - "For breakfast, I don't eat solid food. I only drink a protein shake."
- **解説**: 「プロテイン一杯」は **a protein shake** が自然。食べ物なしは **I don't eat solid food** / **I don't have anything else** で言える。

### ② 「16時間断食が流行ったことをきっかけに、食生活を見直した」
- **言いたかったこと**: 7年くらい前、16時間断食が流行ったことをきっかけに、自分の食生活を見直した。
- **実際の発話**: "it was said that eating food that fasting was really important" / "I realized 16 fasting."
- **完成形の例文2つ**:
  - "About seven years ago, intermittent fasting became popular, and that made me rethink my eating habits."
  - "When 16-hour fasting started getting popular, I tried it and ended up changing my diet."
- **解説**: 「16時間断食」は **16-hour fasting / intermittent fasting**。「きっかけに」は **that made me...** が一番軽い。

### ③ 「筋トレ期は、朝食を食べないという選択肢がなかった」
- **言いたかったこと**: 体を大きくするには3000kcalくらい必要で、朝食を抜く選択肢がなかった。
- **実際の発話**: "I tried to take a lot of calories, over 3000 calories a day. I had no choice. to eat with my lunch."
- **完成形の例文2つ**:
  - "When I was trying to build muscle, I had no choice but to eat breakfast because I needed around 3,000 calories a day."
  - "At that time, skipping breakfast wasn't an option for me because I had to get enough calories to bulk up."
- **解説**: 覚える型は **I had no choice but to do**。今回の主役。`no choice. to...` で切ると文が壊れる。

## 2. 今回のフォーカス弱点（1つだけ）

**弱点: 「健康・栄養」の名詞を、そのまま日本語発想で英語に置き換えている**

| 実際の発話 | 正しい完成形 |
|------------|--------------|
| "my mind of staying healthy increased" | "I became more health-conscious around that time." |
| "eating food that fasting was really important" | "People were saying that intermittent fasting was good for your health." |
| "my nutrition is inefficient for protein" | "I tend not to get enough protein, so I make sure to drink a protein shake every morning." |

**完成形の例文2つ**:
- "I became more health-conscious, so I started paying more attention to my eating habits."
- "I tend not to get enough protein, so I make sure to have a protein shake every morning."

**覚えるルール**: 「健康意識が高まる」は **become more health-conscious**。「栄養が足りない」は **not get enough nutrition/protein**。`nutrition is inefficient` は言わない。

## 3. 意見スピーチの弱点（1つだけ）

**弱点: 時間軸の転換が多い話で、接続語なしに次の時代へ飛んでいる**

1. **自分の根拠**: ja_log では「昔は朝食を食べていた → 7年前に16時間断食 → 4年前にウェイトトレーニング → 2年前から健康重視」と、かなり整理された時間軸を話している。さらに「十六時間断食が流行ったことをきっかけに」「I have no choice は言いたい」と、接続と核フレーズも自覚できている。
2. **現状の発話**: en_log では "After having the dinner, next time my meal is lunch. Then I skipped the 16 fasting. I realized 16 fasting. But after that..." と、時代の変化は出ているが、それぞれの意味がつながらない。聞き手は「なぜ夕食の次が昼食なのか」「何をrealizedしたのか」で迷う。
3. **理想の言い方**:
   - "I used to eat breakfast every day, but about seven years ago, intermittent fasting became popular, and that made me rethink my eating habits."
   - "Later, when I got into weight training, my goal changed. I had to eat more, so skipping breakfast wasn't an option anymore."

**次回の型**: `I used to... / About X years ago... / Later... / These days...` の4本柱で話す。時系列の話は、英語力より先に標識を置いた方が勝てる。
