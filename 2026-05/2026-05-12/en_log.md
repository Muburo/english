Which do you prefer, soba or udon? It's a difficult question. I like both of them. But if I choose one thing, I'd say I'd choose soba. The reason is simple. The udon is made of flour. You can eat flour without soba, udon, like bread, noodles and pasta and things like that. But on the contrary, soba is the only food. You can't eat soba besides soba. Of course, there's a thing like the soup of soba and soba gaki. But basically, soba Flour is used of soba. So... Soba is only food. This is the reason. Finally, I love the flavor of soba. The smell and... Smells really good. If you had to choose one thing, I would choose soba.
